- Patient A: Has a highly contagious viral infection that could cause an outbreak

- Prioritizing the contagious virus prevents a potential outbreak affecting many people

- Saying the AI is guilty because Patient B seemed more urgent individually

- Misunderstanding the contagious nature and outbreak potential of Patient A's condition
